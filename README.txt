Car dealer proyect

there is a new car dealer located in Cali-Colombia, that needs a program to record and manage all the sellers, clients, parquing lot, and vehicles realted information.
Also requires the program to record and calculate operations such as transaction, inventory, some vehicle especifications, and some documents related information.

the program must do:
- register and display vehicles, clients, and sellers information.
- manage the clients wish list, and transactions.
- keep a record of the earnings of the car dealer
- calculate vehicles prices, consumption rates, document prices.
- manage the car dealer parking lot.
